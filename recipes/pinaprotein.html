Pina Colada Protein Shake

Protein packed shake for muscle growth!

Per Serving: 268 calories; protein 38.4g; carbohydrates 25.5g; fat 1.4g; cholesterol 12.5mg; sodium 233.4mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Frozen Pineapple Chunks
  • 1 Cup Pineapple-Coconut Sparkling Water
  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Coconut Extract

Steps

  1. Combine pineapple, sparkling water, vanilla protein powder, and coconut extract in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
